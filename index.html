    COMSOC - UWU

    The Computer Society of Uva Wellassa University of Srilanka is reguarded as the most active society in the university, It is also one of the first societies founded in the university

    Our Mission

    The Mission of our computer society is to serve students with excellence in computer and technological knowledge and we attempt to instil in them the attitudes and values that will prepare them for a lifetime of continued learning and leadership.

    Our Plan

    The plan of our computer society for the accedamic year of 2019/2020 is to organize workshops on the trending technologies used at the industry level and also to conduct competetive codeing sessions to improve the problem solving skills of the students.

    Our Vision

    Helping the students to improve the technical knowledge as well as building their leadership skills and providing knowledge with better preparatuion and increassing the quality of workshops with the influence of industry professionals.

    Services

    Our main objective is to improve the technical skills and building leadership of the students so that they will be better prepaierd for ther future at the industry level

    Application Development

    Organizing workshops to educate students about Rapid Application Develpment and Web Application Development

    Tech Sessions

    Organsing technical sessions with industry professionals to enlighten the students about the latest technologies used at industry level

    Codeing Sessions

    Organizing sessions to educate the students about programming languages and to increase their problem sovling skills

    Hackathons

    Organizing competitive coding competitions such as the Deft Coders to increase the competitive codeing skills of the students

    Mini-Hackathons

    The main intentions of organizing such events is to educate and give practice to students in using codeing platforms

    Technical Wokshops

    Organizing workshops to increase technical knowlegde of students
